System Purpose

A comprehensive platform for organizing and managing debate competitions. Register participants, evaluate with structured scoring, manage schedules, generate rankings, and award professional certificates.

Core Features

Registration

Participant enrollment with email, college, topic selection, and debate side assignment.

Scoring

Judge-based evaluation: Content (30), Delivery (30), Rebuttal (40) = 100 points maximum.

Management

Organize participants, assign schedules, manage lineups, and export data with ease.

Results & Certificates

Real-time rankings, top performer highlights, and professional PDF certificates for all participants.

Why Use This System?

  • Efficient: Automated workflows reduce administrative overhead
  • Accurate: Structured scoring ensures fair evaluation
  • Transparent: Real-time results with complete audit trails
  • Professional: Generates official certificates and documentation
  • Simple: Easy-to-use interface for judges and participants
